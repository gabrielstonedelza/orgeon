How to fill client form

Please take note,whatever you put in the client form is important,not just for you but to all those that would be reading the client's profile.

If you are registering a client for the first time,it signifies that you have already gone via the care plans above and it's safe to say that you know exactly what care plan to pick for the client.

Most of the fields are self explanatory,however it would be confusing ang challenging for you and all the client profile readers if you let's say miss a step or entered a details in the wrong field.

Please follow these steps.

1.Care Plan => Pick a care plan that is suitable for the client's needs.
2.Name => Name of the client
3.Age => How old is the client?
4.Gender => Is the client a male or female?
5.Image => Photo of the client,you should have a photo of the client on your pc,if not at the moment leave this field blank and update later when you get a photo of the client.
6.Next of kin => In case the client is not available ,who should replace him or her
7.Email => If the client has an email,add it and leave blank if they don't have one
8.Phone => Client's personal contact number
9.Emergency Phone => In case the client is not reachable,who to contact
10.Issue => Challenge(s) the client if facing currently
**The following steps matter
11. Progress => As default every new client created is assigned a level or phase of Assessment and that is set as default on the form as you can see.
12. Assessment => This phase is where the client stars his or her journey via the care,whatever process the client was taken via regarding Assessment,please put them here.It's going to be ease for anyone reading the clients profile get the understanding of what tranpired in Assessment phase.
Leave the other phases alone.Only fill or update a phase only when the client has reach that particular phase.

Family Malfunctioning
