Precious Lives(Faustina's Story)


ORgeon of Stars followed up on a murder case on Joy News, a story covered by Afua a journalist from Joy FM station. An employee was murdered by his boss on April,18th 2019, the decease known as Kwabena Sekyere, left behind a pregnant wife with a 2-year-old daughter. Receiving the shocking news, the decease wife went into labor and delivered a baby girl. ORgeon of Stars upon hearing this terrible incident, went to aid the decease’s wife by name Faustina and her 2 children on friday April 26,.2019. We presented a monetary award and will continue our care till mother is stable enough to cater for her children.
